History of Accidents

Did you know that New York City has had over 150k accidents every year for the past 4 years? Click on the dots at the bottom to switch between Yearly/Monthly/Daily views of the data.

Interactive map

Zoom in on any area of the map. In heat map view, warmer colors represent more dangerous intersections. In detailed view, red color markers represent the most dangerous intersections, blue markers represent moderately dangerous intersections, and green markers represent least dangerous intersections. Click on any marker to see more details about that intersection.
